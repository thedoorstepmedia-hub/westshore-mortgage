About Westshore

Independent advice you can actually trust.

Westshore Mortgage Group is an independent brokerage based in Vancouver, BC. Because we're not employed by any bank or lender, our only job is to find the mortgage that's best for you — not the one that earns us the highest commission.

We've helped over 500 Metro Vancouver families buy their first home, renew smarter, and use their home equity to build wealth. From Burnaby to the Fraser Valley, we know this market.

Our service is completely free to borrowers. Lenders pay our fee when a mortgage closes — so you get expert guidance at no cost to you.
